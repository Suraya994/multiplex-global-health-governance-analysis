## Methodological Rule

The dissertation core corpus must include only health-law/public-health/IHR/pandemic/health-rights/TRIPS-public-health primary or official normative texts from 2005-2025.
Energy/logistics texts are excluded from the core unless they are directly health-supply or pandemic-border-management texts; even then they are supportive, not core.
